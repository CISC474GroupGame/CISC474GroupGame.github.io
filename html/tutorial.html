Game Elements

Picture Name Description
Crazy Cube This is the player's controllable character.
Coin Collect coins to gain points. Coins are worth 100 points each.
Wind Crystal Crazy Cube obtains power from the wind, and receives the ability of flying.
Diamond Crystal Crazy Cube obtains power from the pink diamond,
and reduces its body to a smaller size.
Darkness Crystal Crazy Cube obtains the power of darkness. The jump ability is boosted.
Flame Crystal Crazy Cube obtains the power of flame, and it will become invincible.
Key Collect the key to unlock the door. Key must be collected to complete level.
Door Reach the door to complete the level. Door must be unlocked to complete level.
